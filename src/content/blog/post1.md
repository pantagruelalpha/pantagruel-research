## Welcome

This is **Pantagruel Research**, the technical blog of [Pantagruel Alpha SL](https://pantagruel-research.com) — an independent trading firm focused on quantitative strategies, derivatives and systematic investing.

We created this space to share, in long-form detail, the research that drives our day-to-day work: from volatility modelling and derivatives pricing to machine-learning pipelines applied to financial markets.

---

## What to expect

The articles published here will be the direct output of our internal research process. Topics will include, but are not limited to:

- **Derivatives & volatility** — pricing models, Greeks, term-structure dynamics, variance swaps.
- **Systematic strategies** — momentum, mean-reversion, carry, and factor-based portfolio construction.
- **Machine learning for finance** — time-series forecasting, feature engineering, purged cross-validation, and production-grade MLOps.
- **Risk management** — VaR, CVaR, scenario analysis, and stress testing.
- **Mathematical foundations** — stochastic calculus, optimisation, and statistical inference as they apply to real trading problems.

Every post will aim to combine **academic rigour** with **practical implementation** — we will share code, data pipelines and reproducible experiments whenever possible.
